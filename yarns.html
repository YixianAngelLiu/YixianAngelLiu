ABOUT ME

Hello! My full name is Yixian Angel Liu, but call me Angel! I'm a highschooler who sometimes runs along forest trails, bakes burnt goods, and pretends to be a great singer. But most of all, I enjoy designing and coding websites, and exploring new effects and techniques.

I couldn't have begun my journey without the help of my friends at Cumulus, an ed-tech organization focused on providing resources to underprivileged communities. And working through coding stumps alongside my sister made it possible to laugh off our frustrations.

As I look to my future, I truly do hope that I can continue on this fulfilling path.
